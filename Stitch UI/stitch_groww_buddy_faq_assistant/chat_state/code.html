What are the expense ratio limits for equity mutual funds?

auto_awesome
Verified Response

Under SEBI regulations, the maximum Total Expense Ratio (TER) for equity-oriented mutual fund schemes is tiered based on the assets under management (AUM).

  • On the first ₹500 crores of AUM: 2.25%
  • On the next ₹250 crores of AUM: 2.00%
  • On the next ₹1,250 crores of AUM: 1.75%
  • On the next ₹3,000 crores of AUM: 1.60%
link 🔗 Source: SEBI Master Circular 2024 Last updated from sources: 2026-06-10
